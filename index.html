  • Drone Food Delivery Service - Java

    The task of this project was to plan the route that a drone will take to deliver food and drink orders on a given day. There were a number of limitations to this:
    1) The drone had a limited number of moves before it ran out of battery.
    2) The drone could not fly over certain predefined buildings (no-fly zone(s)).
    To find the shortest route, I implemented a nearest neighbour heuristic between adjacent orders based on the straight-line distances between two potential start and destination nodes. Once this was obtained, I found potential rerouting nodes, the vertices of the bounding box for each no-fly zone, so as to avoid the obstacles. However, another limitation is the drone could only fly on a bearing that was a multiple of ten between zero and three hundred and fifty degrees. To ensure that the no-fly zones were never touched, a implemented an algorithm to apply local corrections to the current path whilst ensuring the smallest deviation from the target.
